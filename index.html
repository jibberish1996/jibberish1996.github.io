A Little About Me

Hi, my name is Muhammad Zakir and I'm from Indonesia, I lived in the capital city of Indonesia which some of you might already know that it was Jakarta. I'm currently taking a Bachelor Degree in Information Systems at Gunadarma University. I'm in love with Web Development and I'm a little fans of Security. I'm a kind of a Gamer too in a way, and I'm a fans of an anime called One Piece. I'm an Open Source lover too, I'm a Linux user, to be exactly I'm using Ubuntu GNOME on my notebook for my daily usage.
I'm currently interested in Python, especially Flask for the Web Development side. I'm interested in the two of them because of the power of Python itself and the freedom from Flask. For the Front-End side, I love to use MaterializeCSS because I'm in love with Material Design. Beside all of those things, I like IoT stuff and some other related stuff that i could make use of for a tons of things like Wake on Lan, Expert Systems, Game Development, etc. By the way, feel free to ask me about anything that I might be any kind of help to you or even just to know a little bit more about me.
